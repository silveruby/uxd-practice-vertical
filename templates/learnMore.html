Goal of the Site

The goal of the site is to share the practice verticals with everyone who wants to create their own UX paths. In my journey to define my UX skills, I came across an article called UX Design Practice Verticals. It breaks down UX practices into 8 main practice verticals. Each practice verticals has six area of interests, with each one building on top of the previous. I loved the concept and used it to define my UX path.
